India's Oldest University

The University of Lucknow or Lucknow University is a public state university based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.
Founded in 1867, the University of Lucknow is one of the oldest government owned institutions of higher education in India.

Cource We Offer

Lucknow University is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), Bar Council of India (BCI) and National Council of Teacher Education (NCTE). Established in 1920, Lucknow University is a public state university. University of Lucknow offers over 82 courses at the UG and PG levels. Lucknow University has 9 faculties, including Arts, Unani, Ayurveda, Engineering and Technology, Commerce, Education, Fine Arts, Science and Law. The University has 16 institutes or centres and 30 departments. In addition, as many as 227 colleges have been affiliated with the University of Lucknow to impart top-notch education in Uttar Pradesh.

Our Campus

Our Facilities

Lucknow University facilities include residential facilities, a library, sports facilities, cafeteria, auditorium, medical facilities and many other facilities. The university provides separate hostels for boys and girls. All basic amenities have been provided in the hostels.

World Class Library

Library is the Central Research library of the University of Lucknow. It is here that the academic fraternity- both the preceptor and his pupils could find intellectual food. It is respected as one of the oldest, well organised and richest libraries of India. With the advent of Information Technology, this library is also changing its traditional format.

Largest Play Ground

The old campus of the University boosts of two dedicated fields for outdoor sports activities like football, cricket, volleyball and athletics.

Tasty and Healthy Food

To provide fresh and packaged food, there are five Canteens in the Old Campus and three in the Second Campus. They provide a variety of nutritious and low priced food items to the students, teachers, employees and visitors to the University.
